## Precision control

The complete 182-case matrix was repeated with a second CUDA library compiled without `--use_fast_math`. All 182 GPU scores were numerically identical to the fast-math run, while kernel time increased from 44.764s to 68.504s (+53%). The observed CPU/GPU drift therefore comes from sustained Float32 state and threshold branching, not CUDA fast-math approximations; keeping fast math is the better tradeoff.
